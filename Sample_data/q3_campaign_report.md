# Axiom — Q3 Campaign Performance Report
**Department:** Marketing
**Prepared by:** Maya (Marketing Lead)
**Quarter:** Q3 FY26

## Summary
Q3 focused on two major regional pushes: European expansion and a pilot in Southeast Asia (SEA). Results diverged sharply.

## Campaign Breakdown

### 1. EU Expansion Campaign
- **Channels:** LinkedIn Ads, Google Search, Webinars
- **Spend:** ₹18,00,000
- **Target activation rate:** 4.5%
- **Actual activation rate:** 2.7% (0.6x of target)
- **Verdict:** Underperforming. CAC is 40% higher than company average. Recommend pausing or cutting spend by at least 50% going into Q4.

### 2. SEA Pilot Campaign (Tamil Nadu & Kerala regional push)
- **Channels:** Regional social media, local content partnerships
- **Spend:** ₹6,00,000 (small pilot batch)
- **Target activation rate:** 4.0%
- **Actual activation rate:** 8.1% (2x of target)
- **Verdict:** Strong signal. Regional-language content drove significantly higher engagement than pan-India/generic English campaigns. Recommend scaling this in Q4.

## Key Insight
Regional-language campaigns in South India outperformed English-only campaigns by ~2x on activation. However, scaling this requires **dedicated regional content creators** — current team has zero writers fluent in Tamil or Malayalam.

## Q4 Recommendation
- Cut EU spend by 50%, reallocate to SEA regional push
- Estimated cost to scale SEA campaign to full regional launch: **₹15,00,000**
- Blocker: need 2–3 regional content hires before scaling (see HR open roles)
