# Axiom — Spend Approval Policy
**Department:** Finance
**Prepared by:** Kubera (Finance Lead)

## Core Principle
Speed matters, but every rupee is tracked. Department leads have autonomy within their allocated budget — approval is only required when spend crosses a threshold or requires new budget.

## Approval Thresholds
| Spend Amount | Approval Needed |
|---|---|
| Up to ₹5,00,000 | Department lead only — no additional approval |
| ₹5,00,001 – ₹15,00,000 | Finance sign-off (Kubera) |
| Above ₹15,00,000 | Finance + CEO sign-off |

## Reallocation Rules
- Moving budget **within** a department (e.g. Marketing shifting funds from EU campaign to SEA campaign) does not require new approval, as long as:
  1. Total department spend stays within its quarterly cap
  2. Kubera is notified for tracking purposes (not blocking)
- Moving budget **across** departments always requires Finance approval

## New Hire Spend
- Contractor engagements are treated as operating spend, not headcount — they fall under the same thresholds above
- Full-time hires with new salary budget require Finance + HR joint sign-off before an offer is extended

## Quarterly Review Cadence
- Kubera reviews all department spend every 2 weeks
- Any department projected to exceed its quarterly cap is flagged early, not at quarter-end
