# Axiom — Hiring & Employment Policy
**Department:** HR
**Prepared by:** Vibhishana (People Lead)

## Core Philosophy
Axiom hires lean and moves fast. We prefer small, high-ownership teams over large departments. Every hire must be justified by a clear business need — no headcount for headcount's sake.

## Hiring Process
1. Department lead submits a hiring request with business justification
2. HR reviews against current budget/headcount plan
3. Requests requiring new budget allocation need Finance sign-off (Kubera)
4. Approved roles are posted; target time-to-hire is **3 weeks** for individual contributor roles, **6 weeks** for leadership roles

## Full-Time vs Contractor
- **Full-time hires:** Best for long-term, ongoing needs. Notice period: **30 days** (both sides).
- **Contractors:** Best for time-boxed or pilot initiatives. Can be onboarded in **1–2 weeks**. Preferred route when speed matters more than long-term commitment (e.g. scaling a pilot campaign, short-term content needs).

## Notice Period & Exit
- Full-time employees: 30 days notice period, standard exit process, no non-compete beyond 6 months
- Contractors: Engagement terms defined per contract, typically 1–2 week off-ramp

## Approval Thresholds
- Up to 2 new hires within existing budget: HR + department lead approval only
